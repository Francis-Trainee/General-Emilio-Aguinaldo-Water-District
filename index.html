Welcome to the General Emilio Aguinaldo Water District

Your trusted water service provider, ensuring safe and reliable water for all.

Our Mission

Is to govern, control and oversee safe drinking water from catchment to concessionaire's taps with the premier water transmission and distribution process and system and with the finest disinfection procedure by the energized and objective driven employees.

Our Vision

Is to be a world class, state-of-the art water transmision and distribution system that will sustain 24/7 of uninterruptible supply of safe drinking-water in parallel with the preservation of watershed all throughout the Municipality of Gen. E. Aguinaldo, Cavite.

Our Core Values

Sustainability . Transparency . Stewardship . Customer Service . Innovation
