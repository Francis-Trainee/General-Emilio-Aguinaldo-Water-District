Gen. E. Aguinaldo Water District

Mandate

The GEAWD (hereinafter referred to as "District") upholds the following institutional principles embodied in its Vision, Mission, and Core Values.

Vision

IS TO BE A WORLD-CLASS, STATE-OF-THE-SYSTEM THAT WILL SUSTAIN 24/7 UNINTERRUPTIBLE SUPPLY OF SAFE DRINKING WATER, IN PARALLEL WITH THE PRESERVATION OF THE WATERSHED THROUGHOUT THE MUNICIPALITY OF GEN. E. AGUINALDO, CAVITE.

Mission

IS TO GOVERN, CONTROL, AND OVERSEE SAFE DRINKING WATER FROM CATCHMENT TO CONCESSIONAIRE'S TAPS WITH THE PREMIER WATER TRANSMISSION AND DISTRIBUTION PROCESS AND SYSTEM, USING THE FINEST DISINFECTION PROCEDURE SUPPORTED BY ENERGIZED AND OBJECTIVE-DRIVEN EMPLOYEES.

Core Values

Sustainability: Commit to managing water resources in a way that ensures long-term availability and environmental health.
Transparency: Operate openly and communicate decisions, processes, and data clearly to the public.
Stewardship: Take responsibility for protecting and preserving water resources for current and future generations.
Customer Service: Provide exceptional service, addressing the needs and concerns of the community with respect and efficiency.
Innovation: Embrace new technologies and methods to improve water management and service delivery.
