Gen. E. Aguinaldo Water District

General Emilio Aguinaldo Water District is committed to providing clean and safe water to the community. We strive for excellence in water management and ensure sustainable practices for future generations.

Careers

Water Treatment Plant Operator

Operates and maintains equipment that treats water to make it safe for consumption.

Civil Engineer

Designs and manages construction projects related to water infrastructure, like pipelines and treatment facilities.

Hydrologist

Studies water resources and distribution to ensure sustainable management and address water-related issues.

Environmental Scientist

Monitors and analyzes environmental impacts related to water use and ensures compliance with regulations.

Water Resource Manager

Oversees the allocation and management of water resources to meet community needs.

Field Technician

Conducts maintenance and repair work on water systems and infrastructure.

Administrative Roles

Includes positions like finance, human resources, and customer service to support the district's operations.

Public Relations Specialist

Manages communication with the community, handles public inquiries, and promotes water conservation efforts.
