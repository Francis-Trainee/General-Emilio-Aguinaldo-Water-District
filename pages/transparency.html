    Transparency Seal

    A pearl concealed within a tightly-shut shell holds little value; it is only when it is revealed that its true worth becomes apparent. Similarly, government information is like a pearlâ€”it gains significance only when it is made accessible to the public.

    The Transparency Seal, symbolized by a pearl emerging from an open shell, represents a commitment to increased transparency and openness in government affairs. This emblematic shift encourages civil servants to engage more openly with citizens and invites the public to actively participate in governance.

    This initiative reflects a broader vision of reinforcing the Philippines' role as the "Pearl of the Orient," embodying democratic values and setting a shining example of transparency and accountability in the region.
